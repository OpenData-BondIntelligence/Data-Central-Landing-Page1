What is rel-val

Relative value is a method of determining an asset's worth that takes into account the value of similar assets. This is in contrast with absolute value, which looks only at an asset's intrinsic value and does not compare it to other assets.

What Is a Bond Screener?

A Bond screener is an essential tool used by bond traders and investors to sift through thousands of bonds to find those that best meets their criteria. Users can apply any number of filters based on fundamental and technical metrics to narrow down their list.
